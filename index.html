About AWS

The American Welding Society (AWS) is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the scientific and technological advancement, and application of welding, allied joining and cutting processes. AWS strives to move the industry forward by connecting welding communities from around the world, developing higher standards, and creating career opportunities.

AWS created the Accredited Test Facility (ATF) program. ATFs play an integral part in the operation of the AWS Certified Welding program. It establishes requirements for test facilities, their personnel, and equipment to test and qualify welders.

We are Accredited

Site Skills Training has been an Accredited Test Facility since February 1, 2014. We are one of only two ATFs located in the Philippines. The accreditation recognises our capability to deliver comprehensive assessments, including having a full-time certified welding inspector, and a welding facility fully equipped for assessing individuals in MMAW, GMAW, GTAW, and Arc Gouging.

Talk to Us

Our reputable record in servicing the welding and fabrication sector ensures our capability to develop people that meet the needs of various industries. For more information regarding our AWS Accredited Test Facility, contact us today.
